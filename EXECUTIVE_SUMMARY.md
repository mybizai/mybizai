# Executive Summary

MyBizAI is an ambitious, AI-powered business planning and management platform designed to be an all-in-one solution for entrepreneurs and businesses. It is not merely a boilerplate but a feature-rich application that guides users from idea generation and market research to financial planning and team collaboration.

## Vision & Goal

The core vision of MyBizAI is to leverage artificial intelligence to simplify and enhance the complex process of building and managing a business. The platform aims to provide users with intelligent tools for brainstorming, competitive analysis, financial projections, and marketing strategy, effectively acting as an AI co-pilot for business success.

## Key Features & Scope

The project's scope is extensive, as evidenced by detailed mockups for over 100 distinct features. The platform is designed to include:

*   **AI-Powered Business Tools:** Features for AI-driven brainstorming, business plan generation, competitive analysis, market research visualization, and financial projections.
*   **Comprehensive Project & Team Management:** Tools for project tracking, task delegation, real-time collaboration, team management, and communication.
*   **Core SaaS Functionality:** Secure authentication (2FA, social logins), tiered subscription billing (Stripe), user profiles, and team settings.
*   **Marketing and Growth:** A suite of marketing tools including a campaign planner, referral program, and newsletter signup, supported by public-facing pages like a blog, about us, and webinars.
*   **Rich User Experience:** A highly detailed and user-centric interface with features like a brand identity kit builder, custom report generation, and extensive personalization options.

## Technology Stack

MyBizAI is built on a modern, robust, and scalable technology stack:

*   **Framework:** Next.js (with App Directory)
*   **Monorepo:** Turborepo for efficient code management.
*   **Authentication:** Clerk & NextAuth.js
*   **Database:** PostgreSQL with Prisma
*   **Payments:** Stripe (supporting multiple subscription tiers)
*   **UI:** A custom design system with Shadcn UI, Tailwind CSS, and a branded "mybiz" color palette.
*   **Analytics:** PostHog for product analytics.
*   **Email:** Resend for transactional emails.
*   **Deployment:** Vercel

By integrating a vast array of AI-driven tools with core business management functionalities, MyBizAI is positioned to be a market-leading platform for modern business development.
