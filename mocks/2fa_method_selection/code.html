Two-Factor Authentication

Enhance your account security by enabling 2FA.

Select your preferred method:

Authenticator App Details

  • Highest Security: Generates a unique, time-sensitive code on your device. Not vulnerable to phone number porting attacks.
  • Convenience: Works offline. Once set up, you only need your device with the app.
  • Requirements: A smartphone with an authenticator app installed (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator).

SMS Text Message Details

  • Good Security: Better than no 2FA. Adds an extra layer of protection to your account.
  • Convenience: Easy to set up and use; no separate app needed.
  • Security Note: Potentially vulnerable to SIM swapping or phone number porting attacks. Less secure than an authenticator app.
  • Requirements: A reliable mobile phone number and cellular service.
