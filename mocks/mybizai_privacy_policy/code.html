1. Introduction

Welcome to MyBizAI's Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform. By using MyBizAI, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services. This includes information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and business details. We also collect information automatically, such as your IP address, device information, and usage data. Additionally, we may collect information from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, if you choose to link your accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve MyBizAI. This includes personalizing your experience, providing customer support, and communicating with you about updates and promotions. We may also use your information for research and development purposes, to analyze trends, and to ensure the security of our platform. Your data helps us to deliver a seamless and effective service.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and analytics tools. We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and interests. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Any sharing of information is done with strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We continuously update our security practices to address new threats.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When your information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. Our data retention practices ensure that your information is not kept indefinitely.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also have the right to object to or restrict certain processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the 'Contact Us' section. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. Your control over your personal information is important to us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our platform and updating the 'Last updated' date. Your continued use of MyBizAI after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@mybiza.com. We are committed to addressing your inquiries and resolving any issues promptly. Your feedback is valuable to us as we strive to maintain the highest standards of privacy and data protection.
